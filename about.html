A Journey Through Time.
Our Hotel's Rich History

Embark on a captivating journey through the annals of our hotel's rich history. From its inception, our establishment has been a witness to the evolving tapestry of moments that shaped its identity. Each chapter unfolds a story of dedication, hospitality, and a commitment to providing memorable experiences.

OUR STORY

1991

1991

Built in 1991, our hotel stands as a timeless symbol of elegance. From day one, Alex Michael has been dedicated to providing an exceptional experience. For over three decades, we've remained true to the values of hospitality and unmatched service. Discover the charm of our history, built by Alex Michael with passion and commitment, and relish in the fame embedded in every corner. Welcome to our proud hotel, where we are committed to creating everlasting memories.

1998

The year 1998 was marked by significant expansion and renovation. We took strides to provide more comfort and beauty to our guests. The addition of modern facilities and enhancements to interior design symbolized our commitment to continuous improvement.

2008

In 2008, our hotel received a prestigious award in the hospitality industry. This recognition not only served as an accomplishment for the team but also as a historical milestone reaffirming our commitment to excellence in service and facilities.

2018

2018 became a year of digital transformation. Our hotel introduced innovative technology-based services to enhance guest comfort and experience. Leveraging technological advancements, we continue striving to provide relevant and efficient services.

2024

In the present day, our commitment to excellence remains unwavering. Building upon our rich history, we continue to redefine the standards of luxury and hospitality. Our focus on personalized service, cutting-edge amenities, and sustainable practices ensures that every guest experiences a stay that goes beyond expectations. As we stand in the present, we look forward to writing new chapters in our story.
